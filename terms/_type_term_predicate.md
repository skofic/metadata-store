# `_type_term_predicate`

**`_title`**

Predicate

**`_definition`**

The term type indicating that the term is a predicate — a named relationship type used in the `_predicate` field of graph edge documents.

**`_description`**

A term marked `_type_term_predicate` defines a named relationship that appears in the [`_predicate`](_predicate.md) field of graph [edge](_edge.md) documents. Predicates qualify the nature of the relationship between source and destination nodes and determine how edges are interpreted during graph traversal.
