## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram per cubic metre (kg/m³)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of mass density (mass per unit volume).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Kilogram per cubic metre is the SI coherent unit for volumetric mass density and the pivot for all mass-density conversions in this dictionary. It is used in atmospheric physics (air density ≈ 1.225 kg/m³) and in expressions of soil bulk density.
