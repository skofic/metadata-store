## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Volume

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for three-dimensional spatial extent.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Volume measures the amount of three-dimensional space enclosed by a boundary. The SI coherent unit is the **cubic metre** ([`unit_Volume_m3`](unit_Volume_m3.md)).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L3I0M0H0T0D0`.

Volume units are used in dendrology (crown volume estimation), soil science (sample volumes), and forestry modelling. The litre (L) and millilitre (mL) are included as practical laboratory units.
