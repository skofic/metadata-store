## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Week (wk)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of time equal to 7 days (604 800 seconds).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The week is occasionally used as a temporal aggregation period for remote-sensing composites (e.g., MODIS 8-day composites are close to weekly resolution) or for phenological event reporting.
