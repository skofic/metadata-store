## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Millimetre (mm)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** One thousandth of a metre (0.001 m).

**[Description](_description.md)**

In climate data, precipitation totals are conventionally reported in millimetres of liquid-water equivalent. The millimetre is therefore the standard unit for monthly and annual precipitation sums in CHELSA, WorldClim, and ERA5-Land datasets ingested by the EUFGIS GeoService.
