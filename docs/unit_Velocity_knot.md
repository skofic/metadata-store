## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Knot (kn)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of velocity equal to one nautical mile per hour (exactly 1 852/3 600 m/s ≈ 0.514 44 m/s).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The knot is the standard unit for wind speed and vessel speed in nautical and aviation contexts. It is defined as one international nautical mile (1 852 m) per hour. Included for interoperability with meteorological datasets and instruments that report wind speed in knots.
