## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Gram per square metre (g/m²)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of areal mass density equal to 10⁻³ kg/m².

**[Description](_description.md)**

Gram per square metre is used for fine-scale biomass measurements such as leaf mass per area (LMA), canopy litter biomass, and surface-level deposition rates. It is numerically equal to 0.01 t/ha.
