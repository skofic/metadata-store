## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Mass per area

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for mass distributed over a unit area of surface.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Mass per area (also called areal density or surface density) is the total mass of a substance divided by the area over which it is spread. The SI coherent unit is the **kilogram per square metre** ([`unit_MassPerArea_kg-per-m2`](unit_MassPerArea_kg-per-m2.md), equal to kg·m⁻²).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L-2I0M1H0T0D0`.

In the EUFGIS database, mass per area units are central to biomass reporting. Above-ground biomass from ESA CCI, field measurements, and carbon stock estimates are expressed in tonnes per hectare (t/ha), where 1 t/ha = 0.1 kg/m². Megagram per hectare (Mg/ha) is numerically identical to t/ha and is used in scientific publications. Snow water equivalent is expressed in mm, which equals kg/m².
