## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Centimetre (cm)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** One hundredth of a metre (0.01 m).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The centimetre is the standard unit for body-scale measurements in forestry and ecology. In the EUFGIS database it is used for trunk circumference at breast height (CBH), diameter at breast height (DBH), and soil-profile depth layers.
