## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilometre per hour (km/h)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of velocity equal to 1/3.6 m/s (approximately 0.277 78 m/s).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Kilometre per hour is the standard unit for wind speed in everyday meteorological communication and weather reports. It is also used in some climate datasets and instruments. Conversion: 1 km/h = 1000/3600 m/s = 5/18 m/s ≈ 0.277 78 m/s.
