## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Cubic metre (m³)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of volume, equal to the volume of a cube with sides of one metre.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The cubic metre is the SI coherent unit for volume and the pivot for all volume-unit conversions. In forestry contexts it is used for standing timber volume per hectare (m³/ha) and individual tree stem volume.
