## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Radian (rad)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of plane angle, equal to the angle subtended at the centre of a circle by an arc equal in length to the radius.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The radian is the SI coherent unit for plane angle. It is a dimensionless derived unit (m/m), consistent with the QUDT dimension vector `A0E0L0I0M0H0T0D0`. Radians are used in mathematical formulations of solar geometry, slope calculations, and trigonometric expressions in climate and terrain models.
