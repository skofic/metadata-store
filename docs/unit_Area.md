## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Area

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for two-dimensional spatial extent.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Area measures the size of a two-dimensional region. The SI coherent unit is the **square metre** ([`unit_Area_m2`](unit_Area_m2.md)).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L2I0M0H0T0D0`.

In the EUFGIS database, area units appear in GCU footprint area (m²), sapwood cross-sectional area (cm²), crown projected area (m²), and clone spatial extent (m²). The hectare (ha) and square kilometre (km²) are used for landscape-scale reporting. The acre is included for international interoperability.
