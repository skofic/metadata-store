## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** United Nations

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Namespace for United Nations organisational and statistical identifiers, including General Assembly regional groups and M.49 geographic region codes.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The United Nations namespace collects identifiers derived from UN systems used to classify countries and territories. Currently it covers two classification schemes: the UN General Assembly regional groups (used for electoral and procedural purposes) and the UN Statistics Division M.49 geographic regions (used for statistical reporting).
