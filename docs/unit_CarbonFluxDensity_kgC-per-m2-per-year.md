## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram of carbon per square metre per year (kgC/m²/year)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of carbon flux density equal to 1/31 556 952 kgC/m²/s ≈ 3.169 × 10⁻⁸ kgC/m²/s.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Kilogram of carbon per square metre per year is used for annual net ecosystem production (NEP) and annual carbon budget summaries. It is the standard unit for comparing annual carbon fluxes across sites and ecosystem types in the context of climate change mitigation research.
