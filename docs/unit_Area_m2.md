## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Square metre (m²)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of area, equal to the area of a square with sides of one metre.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The square metre is the SI coherent unit for area and the pivot for all area-unit conversions in this dictionary. In the EUFGIS characterisation database it appears as the unit for sapwood cross-sectional area and clone size.
