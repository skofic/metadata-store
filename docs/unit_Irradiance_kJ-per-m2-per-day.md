## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilojoule per square metre per day (kJ/m²/day)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of daily irradiation (energy per unit area per day) equal to 1 000/86 400 W/m² ≈ 0.011 574 W/m².

**[Description](_description.md)**

Daily solar irradiation (or insolation) is the total radiant energy received per unit area over a day. It is commonly expressed as kilojoules per square metre per day in agrometeorology and ecology.

Conversion: 1 kJ/m²/day = 1000 J / (86 400 s × 1 m²) ≈ 0.011 574 W/m².
