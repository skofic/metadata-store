**[Definition](_definition.md):** Imperial unit of area equal to 4 046.856 4 m².

**[Description](_description.md)**

The international acre is defined as exactly 4 046.856 4 square metres. Included for interoperability with datasets and inventories that report land area in acres, particularly from North American or UK sources.
