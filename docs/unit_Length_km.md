## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilometre (km)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** One thousand metres (1 000 m).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The kilometre is used for geographic distances, spatial resolutions of remote-sensing products, and the spatial extent of gene conservation units (GCUs). CHELSA and WorldClim products are described in terms of arc-minute grids whose ground spacing is quoted in kilometres.
