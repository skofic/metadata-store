## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Gradian (grad)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of plane angle equal to π/200 radians (one hundredth of a right angle).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The gradian (also called gon or grade) divides a full circle into 400 units, so a right angle is exactly 100 grad. It is used in surveying and some European geodetic instruments. Included for interoperability with surveying data.
