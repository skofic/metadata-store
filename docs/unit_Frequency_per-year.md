## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Per year (a⁻¹)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of frequency (event rate) equal to 1/31 556 952 Hz (reciprocal of the mean Gregorian year).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Per-year rates are used for annual change rates. In the EUFGIS GeoService, CHELSA temporal trend layers express temperature trends in °C/year and precipitation trends in mm/year, both of which can be expressed as a quantity multiplied by a per-year frequency when the numerator unit is separated out.
