## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Decade

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Conventional unit of time equal to 10 mean Gregorian years (315 569 520 seconds).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The decade is used to express long-term trend rates, particularly for climate change analysis where temperature or precipitation trends are reported per decade (e.g., +0.3 °C/decade). CHELSA trend layers provide temporal change rates over multi-decadal periods.
