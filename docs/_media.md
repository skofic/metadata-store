## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Media

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Namespace for media presentation variant terms that categorise blob documents by their display format or aspect ratio.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Terms in this namespace serve as enumeration elements of the [`_blob_kind`](_blob_kind.md) controlled vocabulary. They describe how a blob's content is intended to be displayed — for example, in landscape or square orientation — independently of the content type or subject.
