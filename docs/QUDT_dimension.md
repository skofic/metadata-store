## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** QUDT dimensional analysis

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Namespace for QUDT dimensional analysis terms.

**[Description](_description.md)**

QUDT represents the physical dimension of a quantity kind as a vector of signed integer exponents over eight basis dimensions: A (amount of substance), E (electric current), L (length), I (luminous intensity), M (mass), H (thermodynamic temperature), T (time), and D (dimensionless ratio). This namespace groups descriptor terms that describe those dimensional properties, sourced from QUDT's `qudt:dimensionVectorForSI` predicate.
