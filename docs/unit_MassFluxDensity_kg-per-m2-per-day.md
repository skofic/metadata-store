## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram per square metre per day (kg/m²/day)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of mass flux density equal to 1/86 400 kg/m²/s ≈ 1.157 × 10⁻⁵ kg/m²/s.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Daily mass flux density is the practical unit for precipitation rate and evapotranspiration rate in hydrology and ecosystem modelling. For liquid water (density ≈ 1 000 kg/m³), 1 kg/m²/day ≈ 1 mm/day in liquid water equivalent depth, making this unit directly comparable to millimetres per day for water-related fluxes.
