## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram per square metre (kg/m²)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of areal mass density (mass per unit area).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Kilogram per square metre is the SI coherent unit for mass per area and the pivot for all mass-per-area conversions in this dictionary. It is the SI coherent expression for above-ground biomass and carbon stock density. Numerically, 1 kg/m² = 10 t/ha.
