## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Cubic centimetre (cm³)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of volume equal to 10⁻⁶ m³ (numerically equal to 1 mL).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The cubic centimetre is commonly used for small sample volumes in laboratory measurements. It is numerically equivalent to one millilitre (1 cm³ = 1 mL).
