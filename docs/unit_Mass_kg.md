## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram (kg)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI base unit of mass.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The kilogram is defined by fixing the numerical value of the Planck constant to exactly 6.626 070 15 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s. It is the SI coherent unit for mass and the pivot for all mass-unit conversions in this dictionary.

In the EUFGIS context, kilograms appear in mass-flux and mass-per-area expressions (kg C/m²/day for gross primary production, kg/m² for above-ground biomass in SI-coherent form).
