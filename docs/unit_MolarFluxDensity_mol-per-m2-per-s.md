## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Mole per square metre per second (mol/m²/s)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of molar flux density (amount of substance per unit area per unit time).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Mole per square metre per second is the SI coherent unit for molar flux. In plant physiology, **stomatal conductance** (the ease with which gas molecules pass through stomata) is expressed in mol/m²/s for water vapour or CO₂. In the EUFGIS phenotypic dataset, residual stomatal conductance (Gres) is recorded in mmol/m²/s.
