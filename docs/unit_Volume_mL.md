## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Millilitre (mL)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of volume equal to 10⁻⁶ m³ (one thousandth of a litre).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The millilitre is the standard unit for small liquid volumes in laboratory and physiological measurements. It is numerically equivalent to one cubic centimetre (1 mL = 1 cm³).
