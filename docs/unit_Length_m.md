## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Metre (m)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI base unit of length.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The metre is defined by fixing the numerical value of the speed of light in vacuum to exactly 299 792 458 m/s. It is the SI coherent unit for length and the pivot unit for conversion between all other length units in this dictionary.

In the EUFGIS database, metres appear as the primary unit for elevation, tree height, canopy height, crown radius, crown length, and liquid-water-equivalent precipitation depth.
