## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Square centimetre (cm²)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of area equal to 10⁻⁴ m².

**[Description](_description.md)**

The square centimetre is used for small cross-sectional areas. In the EUFGIS phenotypic dataset it appears as the unit for sapwood cross-sectional area (cm²) and Huber value (cm² sapwood per m² leaf area).
