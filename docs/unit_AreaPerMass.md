## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Area per mass

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for surface area per unit mass.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Area per mass expresses how much area is associated with a given mass of material. The SI coherent unit is the **square metre per kilogram** ([`unit_AreaPerMass_m2-per-kg`](unit_AreaPerMass_m2-per-kg.md), equal to m²·kg⁻¹).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L2I0M-1H0T0D0`.

In the EUFGIS database, area per mass units appear in specific leaf area (SLA) measurements — the ratio of projected leaf area to leaf dry mass. SLA is conventionally expressed in cm²/g or cm²/kg. A related unit mm²/mg is used in some publications and is numerically identical to m²/kg. High SLA indicates thin, productive leaves with short lifespan; low SLA indicates thick, long-lived leaves.
