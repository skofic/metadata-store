## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Megajoule per square metre per day (MJ/m²/day)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of daily irradiation equal to 10⁶/86 400 W/m² ≈ 11.574 W/m².

**[Description](_description.md)**

Megajoule per square metre per day is the standard unit for daily solar radiation totals in agrometeorological and reference evapotranspiration calculations (e.g., FAO Penman–Monteith equation). WorldClim solar radiation bioclimatic variables are expressed in this unit.

Conversion: 1 MJ/m²/day = 1 000 000 J / (86 400 s × 1 m²) ≈ 11.574 W/m².
