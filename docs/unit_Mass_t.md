## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Metric tonne (t)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of mass equal to 1 000 kg.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The metric tonne (also called megagram, Mg) is the standard unit for reporting large masses in forestry and carbon accounting. Above-ground biomass is commonly expressed in tonnes per hectare (t/ha), and carbon stocks in tonnes of carbon per hectare (tC/ha). ESA CCI Biomass data ingested by the EUFGIS GeoService uses this unit.
