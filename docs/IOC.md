## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** International Olympic Committee

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Namespace for International Olympic Committee country codes used to identify nations and territories in Olympic competition.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The International Olympic Committee assigns three-letter codes to each National Olympic Committee. These codes are used in all Olympic broadcasts, results, and medal tables. IOC codes broadly follow ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 but differ in a number of cases — for example, Germany is `GER` in IOC but `DEU` in ISO 3166-1.
