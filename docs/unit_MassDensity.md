## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Mass density

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for mass per unit volume.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Mass density (volumetric mass density) is the mass of a substance divided by its volume. The SI coherent unit is the **kilogram per cubic metre** ([`unit_MassDensity_kg-per-m3`](unit_MassDensity_kg-per-m3.md)).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L-3I0M1H0T0D0`.

In the EUFGIS database, mass density appears in wood density measurements, which are conventionally expressed in grams per cubic centimetre (g/cm³). A wood density of 0.5 g/cm³ equals 500 kg/m³.
