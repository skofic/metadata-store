## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Kilogram per square metre per second (kg/m²/s)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of mass flux density (mass flow rate per unit area).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Kilogram per square metre per second is the SI coherent unit for mass flux density and the pivot for all mass-flux-density conversions. It describes the rate at which mass passes through or is deposited on a unit area of surface per unit time. Evapotranspiration and precipitation rate in physical models are expressed in this unit before conversion to more practical units (mm/day).
