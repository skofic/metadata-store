## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Mass

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Quantity kind for the amount of matter in an object.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Mass is one of the seven SI base quantities. The SI coherent unit is the **kilogram** ([`unit_Mass_kg`](unit_Mass_kg.md)).

Dimensional analysis (QUDT): `A0E0L0I0M1H0T0D0`.

In the EUFGIS database, mass units appear in biomass computations (tonnes), leaf dry mass measurements (grams, milligrams), and wood sample weights (grams). The metric tonne (t, equal to 1 Mg = 1000 kg) is widely used in forest biomass reporting.
