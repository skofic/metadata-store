## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Per month (mo⁻¹)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of frequency (event rate) equal to 1/2 629 746 Hz (reciprocal of the mean calendar month).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Per-month rates express monthly accumulation or occurrence frequencies. They appear in monthly-resolved climate trend analyses and in expressions of phenological event rates per month.
