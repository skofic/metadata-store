## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Square centimetre per gram (cm²/g)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of specific area equal to 0.1 m²/kg.

**[Description](_description.md)**

Square centimetre per gram is the conventional unit for specific leaf area (SLA) and leaf mass per area (LMA = 1/SLA) in plant ecology and physiology. Forest and shrubland SLA values typically range from 5 to 200 cm²/g. In the EUFGIS phenotypic characterisation dataset, SLA is recorded in this unit.

Conversion: 1 cm²/g = (10⁻⁴ m²) / (10⁻³ kg) = 0.1 m²/kg.
