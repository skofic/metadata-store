**[Title](_title.md):** Foot (ft)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Imperial unit of length equal to exactly 0.3048 m.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The international foot is defined as exactly 0.3048 metres. Included for interoperability with datasets and instruments that report measurements in the imperial system, particularly those originating from North American sources.
