## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Megagram per hectare (Mg/ha)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of areal mass density numerically identical to tonne per hectare (Mg/ha = t/ha = 0.1 kg/m²).

**[Description](_description.md)**

Megagram per hectare is the formally correct SI-prefixed expression for the same quantity as tonne per hectare (since 1 Mg = 1 t = 1 000 kg). Some scientific publications and carbon accounting standards prefer Mg/ha to t/ha to avoid the informal 'tonne' denomination. Both are numerically identical and have the same conversion factor to kg/m².
