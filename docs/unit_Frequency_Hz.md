## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Hertz (Hz)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** The SI coherent unit of frequency, equal to one cycle per second (s⁻¹).

**[Description](_description.md)**

The hertz is the SI coherent unit for frequency and the pivot for all frequency-unit conversions in this dictionary. In the context of environmental data, frequency most commonly appears in the denominator of rate expressions. Atmospheric radiation frequency and sensor sampling rates are expressed in hertz.
