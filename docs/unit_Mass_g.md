## [Information section](_info.md)

**[Title](_title.md):** Gram (g)

**[Definition](_definition.md):** Unit of mass equal to 10⁻³ kg.

**[Description](_description.md)**

The gram is used for small sample masses in laboratory measurements. In the EUFGIS phenotypic dataset, specific leaf area (SLA) is expressed in cm²/g and wood density in g/cm³, both of which involve grams as the mass unit.
